Guitar

Guitar as been my passion since I was 8. I began my journey as a classical guitarist, and continued my path until I earned a 4th place in the provincial OMFA Competition for Grade 10 classical guitar. I was pitted against 14 other guitarists, many of whom were planning or majoring in classical guitar in university. I then went on to to train as a jazz guitarist with Rob Campbell for a few years.
