Visynth info_outline

Github

visynthclose

visynth is a music generator that generates unique music based on given pictures. It uses Wolfram Mathematica to generate unique edge maps which are then mapped to pre-determined musical patterns which then sends the results to an audio generator, thus producing soothing, relaxing music that's unique to the image.

ccc-with-c is my competitive programming repo, which I've been working on ever since the 11th grade. It contains 600+ solutions to problems across various online judges, ranging from UVa to DM::OJ. Over my years of comeptitive programming, I've learned much about data structures (segment trees, BITs, DSU, etc.), graph theory (Max Flow, Shortest Path, low-linking, Trees), and general programming speed and problem-solving skills.

Github

monchclose

Monch is a work in progress app targeted towards university students who have trouble cooking due to a lack of recipes and inexperience with cooking. The app helps you take inventory of what's in your fridge and recommends a given meal plan for the week or month using a basic scheduling algorithm to properly allocate the given stock in the best manner.
